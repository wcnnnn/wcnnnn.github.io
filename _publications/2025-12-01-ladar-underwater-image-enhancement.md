We propose **LADAR**, a language-adaptive dynamic underwater image enhancement framework that integrates **DSGA** (Dynamic Semantic-Guided Attention), **LSG** (Language-adaptive Semantic Guidance), and **HCM** (Hierarchical Context Module) to incorporate text semantics into image restoration. LADAR achieves state-of-the-art PSNR/SSIM on UIEB, EUVP, and related benchmarks.
