We propose **DELTA**, integrating Modal-Evolving Task Tokens (**METT**), Cross-Modal Distillation (**CMD**), and Dual-Path Task Attention (**DPTA**). DELTA outperforms existing CLIP-based class-incremental learning methods on 9 benchmarks under both zero-base and large-base settings.
