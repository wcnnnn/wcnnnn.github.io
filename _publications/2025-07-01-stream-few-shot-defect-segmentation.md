We propose **STREAM**, a two-stage SAM adaptation framework integrating **CMFR**, **RCPA**, and **AFIA**. Trainable parameters account for only **0.53%** of SAM, with consistent improvements on NEU-Seg, DAGM2007, and FabricDefect-800 under 1-shot and 5-shot settings at **14.1 FPS**.
