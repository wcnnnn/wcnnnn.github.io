We propose **DLR** (Decouple, Localize, Route), a unified framework combining feature disentanglement and adaptive patch routing across **CLIP**, **DINOv2**, and **SAM**.

- **MVTec-AD:** 95.6% Image-AUROC / 93.5% Pixel-AUROC
- **VisA:** 85.5% Image-AUROC / 96.2% Pixel-AUROC
- **Model size:** 4.12M parameters · **Latency:** 180 ms

**Status:** Revise and Reconsider at *Neurocomputing*.
